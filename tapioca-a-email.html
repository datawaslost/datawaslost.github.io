The unique properties of non-GMO tapioca starches deliver cleaner flavour and colour than grain-based starches. Discover a broad range of stability and functionality, desirable soft-gel texture and new levels of process tolerance, mouthfeel, fluidity and texture with Tate & Lyle’s Non-GMO tapioca starches.

With over 100 years of experience in food and beverage formulations, Tate & Lyle’s newest addition of the Chaodee Modified Starch business in Thailand brings tapioca starch application expertise, technical service, education, tools and global reliability to food formulators. Tate & Lyle’s tapioca starches are researched and tested by experts to provide ideal food formulations in various applications.

Enhance the taste of your food product lines with Tate & Lyle tapioca starches:

  • In dairy desserts, yoghurts and bakery cream fillings, try the clean taste and flavor profile of new REZISTA® Max tapioca thickening starches, which have a very high process tolerance to harsh processing conditions such as those endured in yogurt.
  • In processed and cream cheese as well as soft candy, BRIOGEL® gelling starches provide a range of gel strengths, firmness and fluidity in various product applications.
  • In noodles, dumplings, soups and sauces, Chaodee Modified Starch’s thickening starches are manufactured to provide high-quality dough binding properties, storage stability and cooking qualities.
